Questions

Is it actually my face in the photo? ▾

Yes. We only change the area you brush over. Your face, pose, and everything you didn't paint stays pixel-identical.

How long does a generation take? ▾

Usually 10–20 seconds from tapping Generate to your result.

Can I use it on photos of other people? ▾

Yes, as long as you have their permission. We take reports seriously — any non-consensual use is banned and accounts are terminated.

What can't I do? ▾

No nudity, no content that sexualises or depicts minors, no deepfakes of real public figures, no weapons or violence. Our safety filters and human review keep it clean.

Is there an app I can download? ▾

We're a progressive web app — on mobile, tap the menu icon in your browser and hit "Add to Home Screen." It looks and feels native, no App Store needed.

Do credits expire? ▾

Never. Buy once, use whenever.

Can I get a refund? ▾

Unused credits are refundable within 7 days of purchase — full refund policy here. If you're unhappy with a specific result, tap Regenerate and we retry on us.

Do you train AI on my photos? ▾

No. We never train models on your uploads. Your photos are stored only long enough to produce your result and are automatically deleted — see our Privacy Policy.
