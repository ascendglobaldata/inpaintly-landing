FAQ

Do credits expire?⌄

No. Once purchased, your credits stay in your account forever, even if you don't log in for months.

Is this a subscription?⌄

No. You pay once per credit pack. No recurring charges, no auto-renewal.

Can I get a refund?⌄

Yes. Unused credits refundable within 7 days of purchase. Partially used packs refunded pro rata. See our Refund Policy for details.

What happens if an edit doesn't turn out well?⌄

If the edit fails on our side (model error, timeout) we auto-refund the credit. If the edit completes but you don't like it, you can regenerate with the next credit — we'll also comp a free regeneration if the result is clearly broken.

What payment methods do you accept?⌄

All major credit & debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal — handled securely by our merchant of record. We never see your card details.

Are prices in USD?⌄

Yes. All prices listed are in US Dollars. Your card provider may convert at their rate at checkout. Local taxes (VAT, GST, etc.) are added where applicable and shown before you pay.

Need more than 80 credits?⌄

Buy multiple packs — credits just stack. For high-volume or business use, email hello@inpaintly.app and we'll arrange custom pricing.
